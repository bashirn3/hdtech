Our Services - TURNKEY PROJECTS

TURNKEY PROJECTS

Leveraging on our technical skills, experience and strategic partnerships, we develop and deploy customized and ready-to-use solutions to the delight and satisfaction of our clients

Our Turnkey solutions go through the following key stages

  • Design
  • Implementation
  • Deployment and Maintenance
  • Technology Review

HD Consultants will work with your organization to help you design the right solution from the ground up. We start by analyzing your specific business needs, look at your organizational structure and then provide the best solution based upon the software and equipment available for your budget.

In developing solutions we take the following into cognizance:

  • Business needs assessments
  • Infrastructure
  • Budgets
  • Critical Business Connectivity requirements
  • Backup and Disaster Recovery

In order to ensure seamless implementation in client environment, we employ effective change management strategies in bringing the new solution into your business or organization with minimum disruption of service
