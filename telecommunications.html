MPLS NETWORK DESIGN, IMPLEMENTATION & MANAGEMENT

MPLS (Multiprotocol Label Switching) is a standards-based technology used to speed up the delivery of network packets. We offer MPLS-based network implementation and management services for businesses with multiple sites or locations. MPLS is a more efficient way of both routing and forwarding packets so that your data traffic is properly labeled (voice, video, data, etc) and prioritized for the highest quality of service provision.

Our capacity to effectively implement and support an MPLS-based system is further enhanced by our technology partner, ALBEDO Telecom, with whom we serve the telecom industry, power utilities, R&D laboratories and institutions with reliable network implementation, testing, synchronization, and measurement equipment. With the solution we provide, our clients can verify and install infrastructures that support any kind of applications based on voice, video, and data.
